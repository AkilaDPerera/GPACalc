Nano-Processor

UOM - Group of three people | August 2015 to November 2015

In this project we simulated a 4-bit processor capable of executing 4 instructions. Those instructions are data moving operation, addition operation, taking two's complement and Jumping within the instructions. We implemented this processor on Xilinx Spartan-3E Field Programmable Gate Array and we used ISE design suite software to program it.
